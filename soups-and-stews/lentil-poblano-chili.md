# Chili with Lentils, Chocolate & Poblanos

## Ingredients:

- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 large onions, diced
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 poblano peppers, roasted, peeled, seeded, diced
- 2 red bell peppers, diced
- 4 teaspoons ground cumin
- 4 teaspoons chili powder
- 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, adjust to heat preference)
- 2 cups dried green or brown lentils, rinsed
- 2 cans (15 oz each) black beans, drained, rinsed
- 2 cups sweet corn (fresh, frozen, or canned)
- 2 cans (28 oz each) diced tomatoes with juices
- 6–8 cups vegetable broth
- 2–4 tablespoons tomato paste
- 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder or 4 squares (40–60g) dark chocolate (70%+), chopped finely
- 2 tablespoons white or red miso paste
- Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste
- Juice of 1–2 fresh limes (optional)

### Suggested garnishes:
- Fresh cilantro, chopped
- Lime wedges
- Avocado slices or guacamole
- Crumbled queso fresco or shredded cheese
- Toasted pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
- Sour cream or plain Greek yogurt
- Tortilla chips

## Instructions:

1. **Prepare Poblanos:**
   - Roast poblano peppers, then peel, seed, and dice them. Set aside.

2. **Cook Vegetables & Spices:**
   - Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
   - Sauté onions until soft, about 5 minutes.
   - Add garlic, diced poblanos, red bell peppers, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, cinnamon, and optional cayenne pepper. Cook for another 2–3 minutes, stirring frequently.

3. **Add Lentils and Tomatoes:**
   - Add lentils, diced tomatoes with juices, tomato paste, and vegetable broth.
   - Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer uncovered for about 25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lentils begin to soften.

4. **Finish the Chili:**
   - Stir in black beans, corn, cocoa powder (or dark chocolate), and miso paste. Cook gently for another 15–20 minutes until chili thickens and flavors meld.
   - Taste and adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, and fresh lime juice as needed.

5. **Serve:**
   - Serve warm, topped generously with your choice of garnishes.
