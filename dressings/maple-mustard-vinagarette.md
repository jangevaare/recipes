# Maple-Mustard Vinagarette

## Ingredients
- 6 tbsp olive oil
- 4 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 clove garlic
- Pinch of salt
- Freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. **Combine Ingredients**: Combine all ingredients, and run in food processor until smooth
